Why Participate?

National Recognition

Chance to represent our college at the national level.

Showcase Innovation

Team Building

Prizes & Recognition

Team Formation Rules

Each team must have six members with at least one female participant.

Teams should register through the official form before the deadline.

The Internal Hackathon will be based on PPT presentations. The official PPT format is available on the SIH website.

Teams should prepare their presentations according to the specified guidelines.
