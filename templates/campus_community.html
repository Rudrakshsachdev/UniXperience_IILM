Student Clubs & Organizations

Join one of our vibrant student clubs to connect with like-minded peers, develop new skills, and enhance your university experience.

AI Sync Tech

The AI Sync Tech Club is a community for exploring Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies. Guided by our Faculty Mentor Sonam Ma’am and led by President Yug Prakash, the club focuses on learning, innovation, and collaboration through projects, workshops, and events.

150+ Members
Weekly Meetings
Open to All

AIgnite

AIgnite is a student-led initiative that sparks curiosity and innovation in Artificial Intelligence. With Anurag Jain as the Faculty Mentor and Rudraksh Sachdeva as the President, the club inspires students to explore, create, and lead in the world of AI through interactive sessions, projects, and tech-driven activities.

90+ Members
Bi-weekly Sessions
Open to All

Byte Force

Byte Force is the coding club that empowers students to sharpen their programming skills and problem-solving mindset. Under the mentorship of Aarti Chugh Ma’am and the leadership of President Shailesh, the club organizes coding challenges, hackathons, and peer-learning sessions to foster logic, creativity, and innovation in the world of code.

80+ Members
Weekly Workshops
Open to All

ACM

The ACM Student Chapter at IILM University is a hub for students passionate about computer science and technology. Under the mentorship of Preeti Ma’am and the leadership of President Tanvi, the club engages members through coding, research, workshops, and global competitions, empowering students to excel in the world of computing and innovation.
