Important Cybersecurity Notice

Security Advisory

Dear Students,

It has come to our notice that some unknown links and messages are being circulated regarding internships, participation opportunities, etc.

You are strictly advised not to click on or respond to such links/messages as they may compromise your sensitive information.

What to Avoid

Unknown Links

Do not click on links from unknown or unverified sources.

Suspicious Messages

Avoid responding to messages offering unrealistic opportunities.

Sharing Information

Never share personal or login information with unknown parties.

Unexpected Attachments

Do not download attachments from unverified sources.

Safe Practices

Verify Sources

Always verify information through official university channels.

Use Official Channels

Rely on program coordinators and faculty for authentic information.

Protect Accounts

Use strong, unique passwords for your university accounts.

When in Doubt, Ask

Contact IT support if you're unsure about any communication.

Important Notice

Please rely only on official university channels (program & Section coordinators, faculty members etc.) and communications for authentic information regarding internships, events, or any participation opportunities.

Your caution will help keep your data and accounts safe.
